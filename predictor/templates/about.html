Project Overview

BurnAssist AI is a web-based application that uses deep learning to analyze burn injury images and determine the severity of the burn. The system classifies burns into 4 categories — No Burn, 1st Degree, 2nd Degree, and 3rd Degree — and provides immediate first aid recommendations aligned with WHO clinical guidelines and helps users locate nearby medical facilities during emergencies — bringing critical support directly to the user's device when every second counts.

4 Detection Classes

✅ No Burn No significant burn detected. Skin appears normal.
🟢 1st Degree Superficial burn. Redness, minor swelling, no blisters.
🟡 2nd Degree Partial thickness. Blisters, deep redness, intense pain.
🔴 3rd Degree Full thickness. White/charred skin. Emergency care required.

How It Works

  • 1User uploads a burn image or captures one via the live camera
  • 2The image is preprocessed and passed to the AI model for inference
  • 3A VGG16 convolutional neural network classifies burn severity (1st, 2nd, or 3rd degree)
  • 4Confidence score is calculated and displayed alongside the classification
  • 5WHO-aligned first aid instructions are shown based on the detected severity
  • 6Nearby hospitals are located using the device's GPS and OpenStreetMap
