Immediate First Aid

{% if label == "1st_Degree" %}
  • 1Cool under running water for 10–20 minutes immediately
  • 2Remove rings, watches, or tight items before swelling starts
  • 3Apply aloe vera gel or soothing moisturiser to relieve discomfort
  • 4Cover loosely with sterile non-stick gauze if needed
  • ✕Do NOT apply ice, butter, toothpaste, or oil
{% elif label == "2nd_Degree" %}
  • 1Cool immediately with clean running water for at least 20 minutes
  • 2Do NOT break or pop blisters — they protect against infection
  • 3Cover loosely with sterile non-adhesive dressing or clean cling wrap
  • 4Elevate the burned area above heart level to reduce swelling
  • 5Seek medical care if burn is larger than 3 cm or on face/hands/joints
  • ✕Do NOT use fluffy cotton or adhesive bandages directly on wound
{% elif label == "3rd_Degree" %}
  • 1Call emergency services (112/108) immediately — do not delay
  • 2Remove the person from danger only if it is safe to do so
  • 3Cover loosely with a clean dry cloth — do not wrap tightly
  • 4Keep the person lying down, warm and still to prevent shock
  • 5Monitor breathing — be ready to perform CPR if needed
  • ✕Do NOT apply water, ice, cream, or remove clothing stuck to wound
